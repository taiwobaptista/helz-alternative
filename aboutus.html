\\About us\\

Core level values and an amazing team

Helz Consulting is an advisory firm that provides expert guidance on setting up new businesses/ventures and assists with the formation and establishment of companies in Nigeria. We also provide technical and practical support to our clients on all aspects of corporate compliance, corporate governance, administration and general corporate matters. We are well positioned to play a strategic role in helping Nigerian entrepreneurs including small and medium scale enterprises achieve their strategic objectives through our robust suite of corporate service offerings.

To be the foremost corporate service provider in Nigeria, deploying excellence, innovation, technology and specialist skills in providing effective solutions to our clients.
